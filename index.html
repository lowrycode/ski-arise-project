FAQs

Here are the answers to the most common questions that people ask us.

All of the resorts listed have options for daily equipment hire. This cost is sometimes included in the price of your package but not always. If you are unsure, please contact us to check. Either way, we can advise you on the best places to hire your equipment at your chosen resort.

Most of our packages include lift passes. For those that don't, we recommend booking lift passes in advance, especially during peak seasons, as it can save time and there are often discounts available.

This varies depending on the particular package. When browsing the package information, look out for the 'What's included in the package?' section. Most of the resorts have a variety of thriving cafes and restaurants so why not take a break from the slopes and sample the delicious cuisine.

Most ski resorts are accessible by car, train, or shuttle from the nearest airport. Most of our package holidays include shuttle transfers.

Some packages offer access to local events, bars, and nightlife. Specific activities depend on the resort and package. Contact us if you would like to know specific details about a particular package.

The best time for skiing is typically between December and March, depending on the location and snow conditions. High-altitude resorts often have reliable snow into April.

Most ski resorts have websites providing live snow reports and webcams for up-to-date conditions on the slopes.

Many resorts have snowmaking equipment to supplement natural snowfall, and some offer alternative activities if skiing isn't possible. We only use resorts that are known for reliable snow and good skiing conditions.
